I studied management at Cherkasy National University, Spanish at Kyiv Linguistic University and business administration at Edinburgh Business School.

Before the government, I was an entrepreneur. I founded Quadrate 28, built marketing teams on demand and launched a media outlet for entrepreneurs that eventually became MC.today. I learned early that the fastest way to change how something works is to build an alternative and make it undeniable.

In 2019, I joined Ukraine’s newly created Ministry of Digital Transformation at 28, as one of the youngest deputy ministers in the country’s history. What followed was six years of building one of the world’s most ambitious digital states from the inside, under conditions most teams will never face: a pandemic and then a full-scale war.

During that time, I led the development of Diia.Business, Ukraine’s national infrastructure for entrepreneurs and Diia.Education, a national edutainment platform for digital literacy that has reached millions of Ukrainians. I was part of the team that built the Diia ecosystem into a model that governments around the world now study. I architected WINWIN, Ukraine’s Global Innovation Strategy to 2030, establishing the systemic framework for the country’s priority sectors. I built CDTO Campus to grow the people who could actually run a digital state, and co-launched GGTC Kyiv as part of the World Economic Forum’s C4IR Network.

I led Ukraine’s position in the Tallinn Mechanism, the international coalition that has allocated over €241 million to strengthen Ukraine’s cyber resilience. I represented Ukraine in EU accession negotiations on the Digital and Media chapter. I served as Strategic Advisor to the First Deputy Prime Minister of Ukraine.

In January 2026, I was appointed Advisor to the Minister of Defence of Ukraine on a voluntary basis. I also continue to support as Advisor to the Ministry of Digital Transformation on a voluntary basis.

Today I work globally. I advise governments building digital and agentic states, help global investors and tech companies enter Ukraine and support Ukrainian companies going global. I host FORWARD, a podcast at the intersection of innovation, diplomacy and the future of governance. I am writing a book.

I am a Non-Resident Senior Fellow at the Atlantic Council and a Senior Fellow at Friends of Europe. I am a European Young Leader, a Yale Peace Fellow, an ISF Fellow and a contributor to the Berlin Global Dialogue and the Agentic State Paper. I have been recognized by Sifted 100 Women in Tech in Europe, Apolitical Government AI 100, Mastercard Legacy, Vogue Leaders Ukraine, and UP100.

I am deeply Ukrainian. I operate as a citizen of the world. I do not consult. I co-create.
